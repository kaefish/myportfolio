Microscope Brand Design

Microscope's founder wanted to provide an app that gave consumers up-to-date reviews on cosmetics and information on toxicity. However, the company was a start-up and had little to no current branding. Through creative and competitive market research and many iterations, we were able to provide a logo, typeface, and mission statement for the start-up. The colors we chose reflect a natural but beautiful brand tone.


Timeline: 8 weeks, Fall 2018

Role: Graphic Designer and Creative Strategist

Tools: Adobe Illustrator


Fall Concert Promotional Flyer

Another student and I were commissioned to design a poster and pamphlet cover for a fall concert. Our client asked us to design something that mixed both fall and Broadway themes. After many iterations, we chose this design, nicknamed "Pumpkin Head," because the letters of Broadway as well as the kick line demonstrated the show, whereas the pumpkins added a fun fall element.


Timeline: 2 weeks, Spring 2018

Role: Graphic Designer

Tools: Adobe Illustrator
