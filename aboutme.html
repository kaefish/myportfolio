Where I'm at Right Now

I am currently in my third year majoring in User Experience Design at the University of Michigan School of Information with a minor in Spanish. I have past internship experience as a Web UI Designer for a collaborative research platform called 1Cademy.


My Journey to UX

In my sophomore year of high school, I elected to take a graphic design class and immediately fell in love with it. I learned quickly, and would often give myself extra projects, like an antelope with cantaloupe for ears (called a “cantaloupe”). As much as I loved the work, I had trouble finding a way to apply my love for graphic design to the real world, as well as understanding how I would be able to make a career using those skills. I highly value an intellectually stimulating challenge in my work, and I had trouble finding that in graphic design work. Then, in October of my freshman year at UofM, I learned about UX design and the School of Information, and I was hooked. Through this career path, I would be able to take my love for design to the next level by mixing both functionality and aesthetics.
